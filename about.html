About me

Hi there! Since you are here, let me introduce myself. My name is Abdiel Peguero and I’m a Product Designer based in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic.

During my professional journey, I have had the opportunity to collaborate with both startups and large corporations. Some of the industries I´ve worked with are: Fintech, Defi, Web3.0 and a few more industries. The majority of these products have been developed for B2C, SaaS, and B2B markets.

Prior to becoming a Product Designer I spent most of my time creating graphic and video content for multiple companies. That led to studying my Bachelors in Digital Communication. Through my collage career I learned the basics of contemporary art, video editing & production, graphic design, 3D design, web development and UI/UX design.

‘‘My goal as a product designer is to bring revenue to stakeholders through great user experience design, and deliver an outstanding product that meets our users’ and development team’s requirements.”

I enjoy family time, travel, sports, and music. I play guitar at my church every weekend. My ultimate goal is to give my family the best life possible. I value commitment, empathy, and humbleness.
